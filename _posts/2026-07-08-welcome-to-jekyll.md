# Your First Article

Welcome to your new tech blog! This is a sample article to get you started.

## How to Add Articles

To add new articles to your blog:

1. Create a new file in the `_posts` folder
2. Name it using the format: `YYYY-MM-DD-your-article-title.md`
3. Add front matter at the top (title, date, categories)
4. Write your content in Markdown

## Example Article Structure

```markdown
---
layout: post
title: "Your Article Title"
date: 2026-07-08 10:00:00 -0500
categories: category-name
---

# Your Article

Your content here...
```

## What's Next?

- Edit `_config.yml` to customize your blog
- Add more articles to the `_posts` folder
- Customize the theme in your GitHub repository settings

Happy blogging! 🚀
